About me

I am currently an assistant professor at Fuzhou University. I've received the bachelor, master and PhD degree (supervised by Prof. Jihua Zhu) from Xi'an Jiaotong University from 2015, 2018 and 2021, respectively. I joint the Department of Computer Science of Fuzhou University in 2022.

My research area is machin learning and data mining, with special interests in multi-view learning, label distribution learning, and subspace clustering.
